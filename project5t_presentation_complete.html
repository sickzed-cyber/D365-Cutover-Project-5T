Risk Reduction Measures

Blackout Period Mitigation

Risk Reduction Timeline

  • Vendor Delivery Management: Last deliveries accepted on September 22, 2025. Normal operations resume on October 3, 2025
  • Customer Order Fulfillment: Early shipment of customer orders to avoid the blackout period. Prioritize orders with delivery dates during cutover window
  • Design Release Freeze: Freeze on new product designs from September 15, 2025. Designs held in Vault for release into D365 from October 6, 2025
  • Manual Transaction Processing: Templates for urgent transactions during blackout period. Manual entries to be loaded after October 6, 2025

Business Impact Assessment

  • Reduced transaction backlog
  • Minimized customer impact
  • Decreased data entry errors
  • Shorter stabilization period
